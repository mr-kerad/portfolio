Frequently Asked Questions.

How much will my project cost?

Every project is unique, and the cost will vary depending on the scope, project specifications, and deadline. I'll provide custom quotes to ensure that you receive an accurate estimate for your project.

Although it is uncommon for small projects to be less than $2.000,00.

How do I get quote for my project??

To receive an accurate quote for your project, it's important to provide as much information as possible about the project.

If you're still in the early stages and not sure what you need, I'll be happy to discuss your project with you and help you understand the costs involved. My goal is to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of your project costs so you can make informed decisions about moving forward with your project.

What are the payment terms for a project?

I require a 50/25/25 payment structure to the projects, with 50% paid upfront, 25% after demo version on Webflow and the remaining 25% paid after launch. This payment structure may be adjusted based on the size of the project.

I accept payments through Stripe or Crypto, and I'll work with you to choose the payment method that works best for you.

How long does a project take?

Most projects are completed and online within 2 to 8 weeks from the start of the project. However, please keep in mind that this timeline may vary based on the complexity of the project and the amount of feedback provided.

If you need updates or new pages after the website is completed, I'm here to help on an on-demand basis.

Do you work internationally?

Yes. I'm based in Poland but I have clients in the EU and United Kingdom.
